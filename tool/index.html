What is Hip Range?

close

The hip range is the angle between the thigh and the torso. It is important to keep this angle constant during the squat exercise to avoid injuries.

What is Knee Range?

close

The Knee Range is the angle between the thigh and the leg. It is important to keep this angle constant during the squat exercise to avoid injuries.

What is Ankle Range?

close

The Ankle Range is the angle between the foot and the leg. It is important to keep this angle constant during the squat exercise to avoid injuries.
